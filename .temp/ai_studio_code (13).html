CHEMISTRY & NATURE

Pure. Hardened.
Protection.

We finish our premium boardgaming tables with a revolutionary plant-based hardwax oil that delivers both exceptional beauty and environmental responsibility.

Oils penetrate the wood fibers to create outstanding resistance to wear, water, and heat. This is essential protection for tables that endure countless game nights, drink condensation, and the natural oils from players' hands.

[1] DEFINITION: VOC Volatile Organic Compounds are chemicals that evaporate at room temperature, contributing to indoor air pollution. Our 0% VOC finish eliminates off-gassing entirely—no toxic fumes, no lingering odors, just pure, protected wood from the moment it enters your home.
